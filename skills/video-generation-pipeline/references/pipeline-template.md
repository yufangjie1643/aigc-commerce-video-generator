## 9:16 Shot Recipe

| Shot | Duration | Visual | Product Proof | Caption/Voiceover | Generation Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 0-2s | Hook reveal | Product clearly visible | Short promise | Strong first frame |
| 2 | 2-4s | Context or problem | Use-case evidence | Pain point | Keep background simple |
| 3 | 4-7s | Detail/demo | Texture/function label | Feature 1 | Close-up, stable |
| 4 | 7-10s | Use/result | Before/after or try-on | Benefit | Natural motion |
| 5 | 10-13s | Hero recap | Product + result | CTA | Clean packshot |

## Prompt Pack

### Image Prompt

Describe subject, product, scene, light, camera angle, composition, and text-free requirement.

### Video Prompt

Describe motion, timing, shot continuity, camera movement, product constraints, and what must not change.

### TTS / Caption

Write short spoken lines and separate on-screen captions. Avoid unsupported claims.

## QA Gates

- Product is visible in first 2 seconds.
- Each claim has visual evidence.
- Text is readable and not crowded.
- Motion does not distort product identity.
- No accidental logos, watermark imitation, unsafe claims, or impossible effects.
- Asset contract contains product, video, and slice dimensions.
- Video-level and slice-level embedding text omit secrets, signed URLs, and raw media payloads.
